How We Use Data

Any information collected is used solely to improve website performance and user experience. Specifically we use analytics data to understand which problems are most popular, which pages get visited most, and where users come from so we can improve the site.

We do not sell, trade, rent, or transfer your personally identifiable information to any outside parties. Ever.

// 03

Cookies

Our website uses cookies for two purposes: analytics (Google Analytics) and advertising (Google AdSense). Cookies are small files stored in your browser.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. If you disable cookies, some parts of the site may not function correctly, but core problem-solving features will still work.

// 04

Google AdSense & Advertising

CodePrep uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. This is how we fund the site and keep it free for everyone. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out via the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

// 05

Data Storage & Security

Your problem-solving progress (which problems you've solved, your streak, bookmarks) is stored entirely in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

This means your progress is private to you, but it also means if you clear your browser data, your progress will be reset. We recommend noting your solved problems if you plan to switch browsers or devices.

// 06

Third-Party Links & Affiliates

CodePrep contains affiliate links to third-party services such as Udemy courses and web hosting providers. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

We only recommend products we genuinely believe in. Affiliate relationships do not influence our content or reviews. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites.

Children's Privacy

CodePrep is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
